Issue Deep-Dive

An issue I ran into during MP2 was getting Sketch 6 to work as intended. I wanted to draw a sine wave in which its wavelength and amplitude depend on the frequency and amplitude of the sound waves produced by the user dragging their mouse across the screen, respectively. The main difficulty of this was scaling it to fit on the display screen appropriately.

To solve this issue, I had to scale the angle and y accordingly. I achieved this by a simple process of trial and error. Eventually I settled on scaling the angle by a factor of 0.01, and the y by a factor of 200, and this did the trick.
